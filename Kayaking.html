Kayaking Trip

Kayaking is a fun and relaxing way to appreciate nature, enjoy the company of others, excercise and more. In his book Why You Should Try Kayaking, Omar Almasri describes kayaking as a peaceful and delightful experience.

Time and Date

9:00am on Saturday, June 12th.

Location

We will be meeting at the lake located at the end of the road in the park.

What to Bring

  • Water
  • Snacks
  • Sunscreen
  • Swim Suit
  • Footwear
  • Change of Clothes

For those who do not own a kayak, rentals are available. Shuttles to the parking lot will only be provided to those that rented a kayak.
