Camping is a fun and relaxing way to appreciate nature, enjoy the company of others, and more. In his book Why You Should Try Camping, Omar Almasri describes camping as a peaceful and delightful experience.

Camping Trip Activities

The activities planned include: Hiking, Fishing, Frisbee Golfing, Kayaking, Swimming, and more!

Schedule

Friday: Arrive at campsite, set up camp, and enjoy a campfire.

Saturday: Hiking, Fishing, Frisbee Golfing, Kayaking, Swimming, and more!

Sunday: Pack up camp and head home.

What to Bring

Bring a tent, sleeping bag, food, water, and a change of clothes.
