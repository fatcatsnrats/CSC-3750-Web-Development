Activities

Hiking

Enjoy hiking in the beautiful forest trails. The first trail we are hiking is 4.6 miles long and the second optional trail is 6.8 miles long.

Saturday at 7:00 AM

Fishing

Fishing will take place on Crystal Lake where pike, bass, and trout are abundant. Bring your own fishing gear.

Saturday at 10:00 AM

Frisbee Golfing

We will be frisbee golfing at the state park and having a picnic afterwards. Cornhole will also be an available activity for those without frisbees or those who dont want to frisbee golf.

Saturday at 12:00 PM

Kayaking

Appreciate the serene waters and breathtaking scenes while paddling on the lake. Kayaks will be provided for those who do not have their own.

Saturday at 2:00 PM

Swimming

Enjoy the clear waters and have fun splashing with friends! Swimming will also take place at Crystal Lake, but not at the same dock as fishing.

Saturday at 4:00 PM

Bonefires

We will be having bonfires at the campsite when it gets dark. Be sure to bring your own smore making supplies!

Friday and Saturday at sundown.
